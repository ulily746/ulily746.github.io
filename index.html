About Me

I am a researcher interested in biohybrid robotics, focusing on integrating living biological systems with engineered robotic platforms. My work explores how biological components can be combined with synthetic materials to create adaptive, responsive, and efficient robotic systems.

My research interests lie at the intersection of biology, engineering, and robotics, with a particular focus on developing systems that bridge living organisms and artificial devices.

Research Interests

  • Biohybrid robotics
  • Soft robotics
  • Microfluidic sensors
  • Bio-integrated systems

Current Focus

Currently, I am exploring new approaches to combining biological materials with robotic architectures, with the goal of developing novel sensing and actuation mechanisms for next-generation biohybrid robotic platforms.
