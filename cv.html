Summary

Master’s graduate in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering with research experience in nanoparticle-based drug delivery systems for metabolic cancer therapy. Skilled in nanoparticle synthesis, characterization, and biological evaluation across cellular and in vivo models. My research interests focus on integrating organ-on-chip platforms, smart drug delivery systems, and biosensing technologies to enable more precise therapeutic strategies.
