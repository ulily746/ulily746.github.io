Summary

Master’s graduate in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering with research experience in nanoparticle-based drug delivery systems for metabolic cancer therapy. My research focused on developing PLGA-PEG nanocarriers encapsulating metabolic inhibitors to simultaneously disrupt glycolysis and oxidative phosphorylation, thereby overcoming metabolic plasticity and selectively inducing cancer cell death.

Building upon this foundation, I aim to integrate advanced biomedical technologies—including organ-on-chip platforms, smart biosensors, and biomedical microrobotics—with nanomedicine to develop next-generation therapeutic systems for precision medicine.
